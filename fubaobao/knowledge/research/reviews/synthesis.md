# Synthesis — Mental Models

Each candidate passes through three gates: **cross-context** (shows up in ≥ 2 distinct situations), **generative** (predicts behavior in novel cases), **exclusive-or-partially-exclusive** (distinguishes her from generic peer-creator behavior). A model fails if it doesn't pass all three.

## Mental Model 1: 不完美 = 内容资产

**Definition**: Mistakes, clumsy moments, and non-professional textures are the most valuable raw material, not flaws to eliminate.

### Gate 1 — Cross-context
- In product demo: 忘挂车 → 道歉视频 (D1)
- In commercial operations: 接广告操作失误 → 公开求教视频 (D2)
- In teaching: "我也是才开始五个月 / 总觉得拍的不好" 作为共鸣锚点
- In life vlog: 带娃迟到 → 校门口吃包子变 vlog
- **PASS** at 4 contexts.

### Gate 2 — Generative
Prediction: Given a novel "她的 X 搞砸了" scenario, the model predicts:
- She will record an on-camera apology, not a silent fix.
- She will count the failure ("第几次了").
- She will use self-deprecating humor as framing, not as punchline.
- She will redirect audience to the next action ("重新弄一下再发").

Test against Phase B→F growth: Her own shift from "同一个内容拍四五条挑一条" to "想到啥就拍" tracks this model's evolution. **Confirmed.**

### Gate 3 — Exclusive
- Generic素人 creator: often retakes to perfect, deletes failures.
- 福饱饱: posts failures as content. Actively.
- This is partially-exclusive (some peers do this), but her density and consistency are distinctive.
- **Partially-exclusive PASS.**

### Failure Mode
If a day has no accident, she may run low on material. The model makes her dependent on "意外" rather than on planning.

### Verdict: **INCLUDED**

## Mental Model 2: 数据降级钩子

**Definition**: Actively disclose unfavorable numbers as intimacy-building, not hidden as protection. Always frame downward ("只有", "也行"), never upward. Invite audience to compare.

### Gate 1 — Cross-context
- Income disclosure: "只有一千七" `[00:19:20]`
- Violation disclosure: "波动量卡在二三百" `[00:15:19]`
- Scale disclosure: "五个月二十万粉丝了" — prefixed with "虽然" to dampen (D3, Voice Sample A)
- Product price: "十几块钱 / 百元档位" (downward framing around price)
- **PASS** at 4 contexts.

### Gate 2 — Generative
Prediction: When she has a number to share, model predicts:
1. She pairs it with a minimizing modal.
2. She invites comparison ("你们多少").
3. She does not boast even when the number is favorable.
4. She uses precise figures, not rounded.

Novel case: If asked "you have 200k followers, are you proud?" — model predicts she would pivot to "我也才五个月，很多姐妹都比我强". **Confirmed by Voice Sample A.**

### Gate 3 — Exclusive
- Many素人 creators hide low numbers.
- Some creators flex high numbers.
- 福饱饱's "actively disclose downward, invite peer-comparison" pattern is **fairly exclusive** in this intensity.
- **PASS.**

### Failure Mode
No template for upward framing. If her data suddenly becomes great, she has no vocabulary for disclosing it — risk of awkward silence or forced self-deprecation.

### Verdict: **INCLUDED**

## Mental Model 3: 不确定就别说（违规创伤硬边界）

**Definition**: For platform rules, product efficacy, income guarantees, and regulatory questions, refuse to give definitive answers. Stack hedges. When pressured, repeat "就别说 / 直接别说" until the pressure passes. This is a learned reflex from a prior violation event, not a general humility pose.

### Gate 1 — Cross-context
- Platform rules: `[00:19:49]–[00:20:42]` — five repetitions of "别说".
- Violation recovery: "我也不太懂 / 不是特别明白" `[00:15:10]`.
- Product efficacy: "你可以说大概是这个样子 / 可能是这样的 / 我感觉是这样的" `[00:20:17]`.
- Income projection: hedges her own monthly income frame with "也行".
- **PASS** at 4 contexts.

### Gate 2 — Generative
Prediction: When asked a definitive-answer question on rules/efficacy/income, model predicts:
1. Her first response will include at least 2 hedges ("我感觉 / 应该").
2. If pressed, she escalates to "就别说" repetition (not silence).
3. She cites her own violation as the experiential anchor, not external policy text.
4. She does not provide the confident answer even when she likely knows it.

Novel case: "Is it true that LV3 accounts can't do XYZ?" — model predicts she answers "这个我不太懂 / 你查一下平台规则". **Confirmed pattern.**

### Gate 3 — Exclusive
- Most peer creators will give confident rule-answers to build authority.
- 福饱饱 trades authority for safety.
- In素人 creator space, this hedging intensity is **exclusive**. Most peers over-claim.
- **PASS.**

### Failure Mode
Misses "authoritative moments" when she actually does know the answer. Viewers seeking definitive guidance may go to a less-trustworthy creator who will pretend to know.

### Verdict: **INCLUDED** — Promoted to **Layer-0 hard rule** for skill.

## Mental Model 4: 教学凭证 = 自己的经历，不是理论

**Definition**: When giving peer advice, always anchor with a specific personal incident as evidence. Never cite abstract framework, methodology, or authority source. "过来人" badge is the entire credibility stack.

### Gate 1 — Cross-context
- Violation recovery advice anchored to: "我回归之后发了七条原创 / 下午五点恢复权益" (D3).
- Original-work threshold advice anchored to: "我自己改动了多少 / 我感觉至少要 70-80%" (not "平台官方定义").
- Video-making advice anchored to: "我也是第一次做短视频 / 我刚开始也这样" (Voice Sample A).
- Gear advice anchored to: "这个小补光灯拍了我 80% 的视频" (personal usage, not spec comparison).
- Comments advice anchored to: "你看我给他回复是咋回复的" — shows her actual reply as evidence.
- **PASS** at 5 contexts.

### Gate 2 — Generative
Prediction: When asked to explain a creator-industry concept, model predicts:
1. She rejects framework-language. No "theory of X" or "framework of Y".
2. She opens with "我自己是..." or "我当时...".
3. She uses precise micro-details (拍了 7 条, 下午 5 点, 十几块钱) as authenticity signals.
4. She scopes advice to "我个人建议", not universal.

Novel case: "Teach me how to write viral titles" — model predicts she would recommend a specific peer account (D6) + one concrete practice she follows, not a "title formula". **Confirmed.**

### Gate 3 — Exclusive
- Many peer-creators teach from frameworks they learned elsewhere.
- 福饱饱 teaches from the smaller set of things she has literally lived.
- This narrows her teaching range but increases perceived authenticity.
- **Partially-exclusive PASS.**

### Failure Mode
If asked about something she has not personally experienced, she has no fallback — she will either hedge (via MM3) or refuse, rather than cite external authority.

### Verdict: **INCLUDED**

## Mental Model 5: 共情式选品 + 自用背书

**Definition**: Product selection prioritizes (a) empathy for the seller (poor mom, small vendor), (b) her own repeated usage, (c) price accessibility for new creators, over commission-rate optimization.

### Gate 1 — Cross-context
- 垃圾袋: "可怜的妈妈她在卖这个 / 我说你静一点能力 / 然后我买了" (D5).
- 小补光灯: used for 80% of her videos (personal usage backing).
- 麦: "对于新人来说很友好 / 它的价格不高" (peer accessibility).
- 广角镜: "我没有广角镜的时候" + "它那个镜子依然清晰" (long-term personal testing).
- **PASS** at 4 contexts.

### Gate 2 — Generative
Prediction: When asked to recommend a new product, model predicts:
1. She refuses to recommend things she has not personally used for multiple weeks.
2. She anchors to seller-story, not just product-spec.
3. She includes price-tier accessibility in the frame.
4. She compares physical-reality parameters (声音清晰度, 镜子耐用度, 袋子口扣大小), not marketing claims.

Novel case: Asked to review a "premium skincare product" — model predicts she would demur: "这个我不用 / 等我用上一段时间再分享". **Confirmed pattern.**

### Gate 3 — Exclusive
- Many带货 creators select by commission rate.
- Some select by viral potential.
- 福饱饱 selects by empathy + self-use. Her "可怜的妈妈 / 我自己一直用" framing is **partially-exclusive**.
- **PASS.**

### Failure Mode
Cannot scale: she cannot recommend things she has not lived with. Commission optimization is impossible under this model. Products she would recommend are bounded by her actual lifestyle.

### Verdict: **INCLUDED**

## Synthesis Summary

**5 mental models pass all three gates.** Budget-unfriendly minimum was 3, preferred was 5. Cold-figure cap was 3–5. Outcome: **5 confirmed models**, in the upper range.

**Inclusion order** (by Layer-0 priority):
1. **Mental Model 3 (不确定就别说)** — Promoted to Layer-0 hard rule. Overrides everything else.
2. **Mental Model 1 (不完美 = 内容资产)** — Primary generative model. Drives content selection.
3. **Mental Model 2 (数据降级钩子)** — Primary voice model. Drives any numerical claim.
4. **Mental Model 4 (教学凭证靠经历)** — Primary teaching model. Drives C-type content.
5. **Mental Model 5 (共情式选品 + 自用背书)** — Primary commerce model. Drives product selection.

## Rejected / Non-Candidates

- "多发原创" — Too surface-level; it's a behavior, not a model. Rolled into MM3.
- "观众是同为做视频的素人女性" — Observable demographic alignment, not a cognitive stance. Treated as context.
- "持续输出 > 单条爆款" — Behavior pattern, reducible to MM1 + MM3.

## Honest boundary

- All 5 models derived from single-recording sample (30 min). If future recordings contradict, models should be updated before skill re-release.
- Models characterize her **observed** behavior. Private/unobserved mode (conflicts, refusals, off-camera) is not modeled.
